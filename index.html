What's the importance
of staking?

The project needs to motivate holders to stake their NFTs for an important reason.
When holders stake their NFTs, it helps to reduce selling pressure by decreasing the number of NFTs available for listing, which can prevent a sudden drop in price



By rewarding staking, the project encourages holders to keep their NFTs locked up and not sell them all at once. This helps to decrease the number of NFTs available for sale, which increases their value.
By motivating holders to stake, the project can create a stable and valuable ecosystem for all holders.
